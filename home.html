About Us

At Little Bird Toys, we believe in the magic of play. What started as a small family hobby has grown into a trusted source for handmade toys that spark creativity and joy. Every item we craft is rooted in love, tradition, and a commitment to timeless quality.

We prioritize sustainability by using eco-friendly, non-toxic materials that are safe for children and kind to the planet. Whether you're looking for a unique gift or a cherished keepsake, Little Bird Toys offers something special for every family, everywhere.
